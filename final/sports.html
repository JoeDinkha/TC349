Soccer

I enjoy playing and watching Soccer the most of any sport. It is one that I feel can connect to more types of people than any sport. It comes from all over the world. I have been playing since my freshman year of high school and it has changed my life. I worked hard on my skills to get better and it helped me to do the same with other things.

Basketball

Another sport I enjoy playing on a nice summer day. Grab a few of your buddies and a game of 21 can become the activity for the entire day. It gets competitive hitting shots in your friends faces or making them fall down with some quick foot action.

Hockey

Not only is this my favorite sport to watch but I enjoy playing floor or roller hockey whenever it is available. This easily takes more short-spurt energy and is very rewarding when a nice play is made or a goal is scored. Defintely a gym class favorite of mine.

Strength Training

Something I have adapted to my life for almost two years now is weight lifting. This is a daily thing I do that helps me relieve stress and prove myself wrong. If you ever want to test your thresholds or thoughts of what you're capable of, this is a great sport to pick up.
